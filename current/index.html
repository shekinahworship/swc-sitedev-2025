What Does Christian Maturity Look Like?

In Paul’s letter to the Ephesians he writes that we should “all come to the unity of the faith and of the knowledge of the Son of God, to a perfect man, to the measure of the stature of the fullness of Christ.” Ephesians 4:13. God calls all believers to grow to full maturity in Christ. This 8 CD series clearly lays out five characteristics of the mature Christian.

Foundational Doctrines of Christ

A massive harvest of new believers is coming into the kingdom. Believers must be discipled and we must be equipped to disciple them. This manual was designed for both purposes. Diving deep into the foundational doctrines of Christ as outlined in Hebrews 6:1-3, these teachings guide believers toward an unshakeable faith and full maturity in Christ. The manual contains 15 chapters and links to 15 corresponding teaching videos.
